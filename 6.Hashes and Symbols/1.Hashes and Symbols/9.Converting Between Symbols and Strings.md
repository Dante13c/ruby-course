Hashes and Symbols

### Converting Between Symbols and Strings

6 min

Converting between [strings](https://www.codecademy.com/resources/docs/ruby/strings) and symbols is a snap.

```
:sasquatch.to_s
# ==> "sasquatch"

"sasquatch".to_sym
# ==> :sasquatch
```

The `.to_s` and `.to_sym` methods are what you’re looking for!
